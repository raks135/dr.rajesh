📝 Gender-Affirmative Healthcare in Nepal

← Back to Home

We're making history with inclusive care for LGBTQI+ patients. Partnering with Blue Diamond Society and Kathmandu Medical College, I helped implement a gender-affirmative healthcare policy.

We trained staff on appropriate pronoun usage, confidentiality, and sensitive health screening for trans and queer patients. We also offered free counseling services.

Healthcare must evolve with compassion, and this experience gave me hope that Nepal is moving toward dignity and respect for all patients.
